    About

    Tokenmaxxing Quest is a satirical incremental game about a software engineer at a big tech company that rewards burning LLM tokens as a proxy for productivity. Send prompts, automate agents, certify pricier models, and shape your org's alignment until one of three board strategies ends the run.

    It runs entirely in your browser — plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, no game engine and no backend. Progress saves locally.
